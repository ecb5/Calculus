Problem 21.7.0.26.

Perform the substitution \(z=\frac{x-100}{15}\) to \(\frac{1}{15\sqrt{2\pi }}\int^{140}_{x=-\infty}{e^{-\frac{1}{2}{\left(\frac{x-100}{15}\right)}^2}}\dx{x}\) to obtain
\begin{equation*} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}}\int{^8/3}_{z=-\infty }{e^{-\frac{1}{2}z^2}}dz\text{.} \end{equation*}
