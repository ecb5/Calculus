Properties of the Definite Integral.

\begin{align} \int_{x=a}^b0\dx{x}\amp{}=0\tag{20.5}\\ \int_{x=a}^af(x)\dx{x}\amp{}=0\tag{20.6}\\ \int_{x=a}^bcf(x)\dx{x}\amp{}=c\int_{x=a}^bf(x)\dx{x}\tag{20.7}\\ \int_{x=a}^bf(x)\dx{x}\amp{}=-\int_{x=b}^af(x)\dx{x}\tag{20.8}\\ \int_{x=a}^bf(x)+g(x) \dx{x} \amp{}=\int_{x=a}^bf(x)\dx{x} + \int_{x=a}^bg(x)\dx{x}\tag{20.9}\\ \int_{x=a}^bf(x)\dx{x}{}\amp{}=\int_{x=a}^cf(x)\dx{x}+\int_{x=c}^bf(x)\dx{x}\tag{20.10} \end{align}
