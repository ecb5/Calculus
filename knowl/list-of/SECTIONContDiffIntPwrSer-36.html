Corollary 25.6.0.9.

Suppose we have a power series \(\sum^{\infty }_{n=0}{a_nx^n}\) converging uniformly to \(f(x)\) on some interval containing \(0\) and \(x\text{.}\)
🔗
Then
\begin{equation*} \int^x_{t=0}{f\left(t\right)dt}=\sum^{\infty }_{n=0}{a_n\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}} \end{equation*}
