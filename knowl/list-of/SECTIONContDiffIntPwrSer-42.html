Hint.
Let \(a\) be an arbitrary fixed point in \(I\) and let \(x\in I\text{.}\) By the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus, we have
\begin{equation*} \int^x_{t=a}{f^\prime{}_n(t)}dt=f_n\left(x\right)-f_n(a) \end{equation*}
Take the limit of both sides and then differentiate with respect to \(x\text{.}\)]
