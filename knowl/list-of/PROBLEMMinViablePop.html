Problem 11.1.1.9.

Of course there is also a risk from underpopulation which model (11.6) does not account for. To catch this effect we tweak equation (11.6) by inserting the factor \(\left(1-\frac{m}{P}\right)\) where \(m\lt C\) is a small positive constant. We justify this tweak in Problem 11.1.1.9 below.
🔗
Our new model is then
\begin{equation} \dfdx{P}{t}=rP\left(1-\frac{m}{P}\right)\left(1-\frac{P}{C}\right)=\frac{r}{C}(P-m)(C-P)\tag{11.7} \end{equation}
🔗
