Problem 14.1.1.12.

Suppose \(g(x)\neq0\) near \(x=a\) and \(\tlimit{x}{a}{\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}}\) exists. Use Theorem 14.1.1.2 to show that if \(\limit{x}{a}{g(x)}=0\) then \(\limit{x}{a}{f(x)}=0\text{.}\)
🔗
Hint.
Consider \(f(x)=\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\cdot g(x)\) for \(x\) near \(a\text{.}\)
