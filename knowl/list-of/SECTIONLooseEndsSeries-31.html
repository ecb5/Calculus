Theorem 25.7.0.8. Extreme Value Theorem.

If \(f(t)\) is continuous on a closed bounded interval \([a,b]\text{,}\) then it has a global minimum and maximum. That is, there are \(c_0,c_1\in [a,b]\) with
\begin{equation*} f\left(c_0\right)\le f\left(t\right)\le f\left(c\right) \end{equation*}
for all \(t\in [a,b]\text{.}\)
