DIGRESSION: How Does Anyone Think of These Things?

Although the substitution we suggested at the beginning of Problem 18.2.0.8 worked it was almost certainly not clear that it would work until it finally did. We (the authors) do not know who first tried to make such a substitution or why, but we believe that it was probably just a guess, at first.
🔗
This is the way mathematics works. Indeed, it is the way problem–solving of any kind works. If you don’t know what else to do you take your best guess and use that to learn more about the problem. We discussed this in DIGRESSION: The Art of Guessing Effectively.
