Example 20.3.0.2. Some Examples.

(a)

\(\int_{x=b}^ax^2\dx{x}=\ftceval{\frac{x^3}{3}}{x}{b}{a} =-\left(\frac{b^3}{3}-\frac{a^3}{3}\right)\)
🔗
🔗

(b)

\(\int_{x=b}^ax^2\dx{x} =-\left(\ftceval{\frac{x^3}{3}}{x}{a}{b}\right) =-\int_{x=a}^bx^2\dx{x}\)
🔗
🔗

(c)

\(\int_{x=a}^ax^2\dx{x}=\ftceval{\frac{x^3}{3}}{a}{a}{b}=\frac{a^3}{3}-\frac{a^3}{3}=0\)
🔗
🔗

(d)

\begin{align*} \int_{x=a}^bx^2\dx{x}+\int_{x=b}^cx^2\dx{x}\amp{}=\ftceval{\frac{x^3}{3}}{x}{a}{b}+\ftceval{\frac{x^3}{x}}{3}{b}{c}\\ \amp{} =\left(\frac{b^3}{3}-\frac{a^3}{3}\right)+\left(\frac{c^3}{3}-\frac{b^3}{3}\right)\\ \amp{}=\frac{c^3}{3}-\frac{a^3}{3}\\ \amp{}=\ftceval{\frac{x^3}{3}}{x}{a}{c} \end{align*}
