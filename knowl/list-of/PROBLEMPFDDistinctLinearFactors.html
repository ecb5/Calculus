Problem A.12.0.4.

Use the HCUM to compute the coefficients of the PFD indicated.
🔗

(a)

Find \(B\text{,}\) \(C\text{,}\) and \(F\text{.}\)
\begin{align*} \frac{1}{x^2(x-1)(x-3)^3}=\amp{} \frac{Ax+B}{x^2} + \frac{C}{x-1}\\ \amp{}+ \frac{D(x-3)^2+E(x-3)+F}{(x-3)^3} \end{align*}
Explain why the HCUM can not be used to find \(A\text{,}\) \(D\text{,}\) or \(E\text{.}\)
🔗
🔗

(b)

Find \(B\) and \(D\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \displaystyle \frac{x}{(x-1)^2(x+2)^2}= \frac{A(x-1)+B}{(x-1)^2} +\frac{C(x+2)+D}{(x+2)^2} \end{equation*}
Explain why the HCUM can not be used to find \(A\text{,}\) or \(C\text{.}\)
🔗
🔗

(c)

Find \(C\text{.}\)
\begin{equation*} \frac{x-2}{(x+1)^3}=\frac{A}{x+1} + \frac{B}{(x+1)^2} + \frac{C}{(x+1)^3} \end{equation*}
Explain why the HCUM can not be used to find \(A\text{,}\) or \(B\text{.}\)
🔗
🔗

(d)

Explain as clearly as you can from the evidence in parts (a), (b), and (c) which kinds of coefficients we can compute using the HCUM.
