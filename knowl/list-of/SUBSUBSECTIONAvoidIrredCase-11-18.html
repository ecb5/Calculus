Problem A.13.1.10.

Prove that if \(z\) is a root of
\begin{equation*} p\left(x\right)=a_0+a_1x+a_2x^2+\dots +a_nx^n\text{,} \end{equation*}
then \(\bar{z}\) is also a root.
🔗
Hint.
If z is a root, then \(a_0+a_1z+a_2z^2+\dots +a_nz^n=0\text{.}\) Take the conjugate of this and use properties in Theorem A.13.1.8. Also note that any real number is its own conjugate.
