Theorem 17.4.2.3. The Squeeze Theorem at Negative Infinity.

If
\begin{equation*} \alpha(x)\le f(x)\le \beta(x) \end{equation*}
on some interval, \((-\infty,c)\) and
\begin{equation*} \limit{x}{-\infty}{\alpha(x)} = \limit{x}{-\infty}{\beta(x)} = L \end{equation*}
then
\begin{equation*} \limit{x}{-\infty}{f(x)} = L \end{equation*}
also.
