Problem 21.5.0.5.

It would have been tedious to compute the length of circumference of the entire circle in Problem 21.5.0.4. Do you see why?
🔗
Use the parameterization \(x=\cos( \theta )\text{,}\) \(y=\sin (\theta )\text{,}\) \(0\le \theta \le 2\pi \) to verify that the circumference of the unit circle is \(2\pi \text{.}\)
