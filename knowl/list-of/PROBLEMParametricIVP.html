Problem 5.9.0.9.

The red curve in the sketch above is parameterized by
\begin{equation*} P(t)= \ParamEqThree{\frac{1}{\sqrt{t^2-1}}} {\frac{t^2-2\sqrt{t^2-1}}{\sqrt{t^2-1}}} {1\lt t\lt \infty}. \end{equation*}
Differentiating \(x\) and \(y\) gives
\begin{equation*} \dfdx{x}{t}= \frac{-t}{(t^2-1)^{\frac32}} \end{equation*}
and
\begin{equation*} \dfdx{y}{t}= \frac{t^3-2t}{(t^2-1)^{\frac32}} \end{equation*}
so that \(\dfdx{y}{x} = 1-(t^2-1)\text{.}\)
