Problem 21.2.0.7. Archimedes Result By Modern Methods.

In this problem we will use the full power of Analytic Geometry, Algebra, and a little bit of Calculus to derive the results that Archimedes had to obtain with considerably more labor and ingenuity using only Geometry.
