Problem 6.3.0.11.

Assume that \(x=x(t)\) and \(y=y(t)\text{.}\) Find an equation relating \(\dx{x}\) and \(\dx{y}\text{.}\) Use this to compute \(\dfdx{y}{x}\text{,}\) \(\dfdx{y}{t}\text{,}\) \(\dfdx{x}{y}\text{,}\) and \(\dfdx{x}{t}\text{.}\)
🔗

(a)

\(\tan(x^2+y)=y\sec(x)\)
🔗
🔗

(b)

\(y^2\sqrt{1+\csc^2(x)}=y^2+x\)
🔗
🔗

(c)

\(\cot(y)=x\)
🔗
🔗

(d)

\(\sec^2(y+x)=y\csc(x^2)\)
