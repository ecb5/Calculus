Problem 25.4.0.6.

Suppose \(\sum^{\infty }_{n=0}{a_n}\) converges absolutely. Show that any rearrangement \(\sum^{\infty }_{n=0}{b_n}\) converges to the same value.
🔗
Hint.
We know that \(\sum^{\infty }_{n=0}{a_n}=\sum^{\infty }_{n=0}{\left(a_n+\abs{a_n}\right)}-\sum^{\infty }_{n=0}{|a_n|}\) and that these series have nonnegative terms. By the previous problem any rearrangement of these must converge to the same values.]
