Theorem 25.3.0.10. The Limit Comparison Test.

Suppose \(a_n,b_n>0\) and
\begin{equation*} \limit{n}{ \infty}{ \frac{a_n}{b_n}}=c\text{ where }0\lt{}c\lt{}\infty \end{equation*}
Then \(\sum{a_n}\) and \(\sum{b_n}\) either both converge or both diverge. That is if one of them converges, the other does and if one of them diverges, the other one does.
